General Information on the Study

This is our time use study.

Time-Saving Feature: You do not need to start from scratch every day. After you complete the first day, your schedule will automatically copy over to the next day as a template. Please adapt this template to reflect any changes in your activities for that day.

How to fill out the diary

You will see a list of activities at the bottom of the screen. You can drag and drop these activities onto the timeline to indicate what you were doing throughout the day.

You will enter information in two steps for each day:

  • Main Activity (Level 1) Drag and drop activities from the activities list onto the time line to show your main activity.
  • Secondary Activity (Level 2) Use this second timeline only if you were doing two things at once. This is for activities that were happening in the background or simultaneously with your Main Activity, e.g.:
    • Media Multitasking: If you were using a second form of media (e.g., listening to a Podcast while Gaming, or checking Social Media while watching TV).
    • Other Multitasking: If you were doing a non-digital activity simultaneously (e.g., Reading while Commuting, or Knitting while watching TV).
    • Note: If you were focused on only one thing, leave this level blank for that time block.
